About Us

Founded in 2008, Kitchen Renovations Werribee began with a simple mission: to bring exceptional kitchen design and craftsmanship to local homeowners. What started as a small family business has grown into Werribee's most trusted kitchen renovation company, having completed over 1,000 successful projects.

Our core values of integrity, innovation, and customer satisfaction drive everything we do. We believe in creating spaces that not only look beautiful but also enhance the way families live and interact. Every project is approached with meticulous attention to detail and a commitment to excellence that has earned us numerous industry awards and countless satisfied customers.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical kitchen renovation take?

Most projects are completed within 4-6 weeks, depending on the scope of work. We'll provide a detailed timeline during the consultation phase.

What is your pricing structure?

We offer transparent, fixed-price quotes based on your specific requirements. Typical renovations range from $15,000 to $50,000+, with flexible payment plans available.

Do you handle all aspects of the renovation?

Yes, we manage everything from design to completion, including permits, demolition, installation, plumbing, electrical, and final finishing touches.

What warranties do you offer?

We provide a 10-year warranty on workmanship and installation, plus manufacturer warranties on all products and materials used.

Can I use my own appliances?

Absolutely! We can work with your existing appliances or help you select and install new ones that match your design and budget.
